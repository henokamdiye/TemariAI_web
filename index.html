About TemariAI

TemariAI is more than an app; it is a high-performance educational environment designed for the modern student. Developed by Henok Amdiye, it integrates advanced AI interactions with social learning dynamics to create a safe, impactful, and innovative space for academic growth.

The Learning Hub

Focus Hub

Collaborative study groups with real-time discussion and academic support.

Social Hub

Educational social media. Post content, interact with peers, and message privately.

Library

Advanced file exchange system for sharing and downloading study materials.

Quiz Center

Beautiful UI for grade-level quizzes with an integrated XP reward system.

Ranked Battle

1v1 live quiz competitions. Climb the leaderboard and earn your rank.

Scholarship Masterlist

Verified, curated global scholarship links updated in real-time.

  • Full GDPR compliance and secure data handling.
